✦   from me, to you   ✦

We never met in a room,
never shared the same sky —
yet somehow, across every screen and silence,
you became one of my very few lovables.

You are the kind of soul that stays —
not in photographs, not in places,
but quietly, in the warmest corner of memory.

Will miss you, maicha…
but missing you means
you were real, and beautiful, and mine to know.

— written with a full heart, always
